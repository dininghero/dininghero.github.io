How it works

  • Set time sensitive menus
  • Simple addition/removal of menu items
  • Enables direct communication between customers and a designated restaurant employee (questions/concerns)
  • Saved logs of user/restaurant interactions
  • Automated waitlists with customers checking themselves in
  • Display restaurant diningroom details including tables, locations, sizes
  • Automated Billing if paid through credit card option
